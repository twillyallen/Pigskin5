About Pigskin5

Pigskin5 is a free daily NFL trivia game: 5 questions, 15 seconds per question, and a daily leaderboard that rewards both accuracy and speed.

Who Runs Pigskin5

Pigskin5 is independently created and operated by Thomas Allen (also known online as Twillyallen). I built Pigskin5 as a passion project to practice Web Development and combine it with my lifelong love of NFL football, and I’m committed to keeping it accurate, fun, and improving over time.

For questions, corrections, or business inquiries, reach me at pigskin5game@gmail.com or through the Contact Page.

What is Pigskin5?

Every day, Pigskin5 publishes one new quiz designed to feel fast, competitive, and “shareable.” You can play on mobile or desktop, submit your score to the leaderboard, and track streaks locally in your browser.

How It Works

  • One quiz per day (same quiz for everyone).
  • 5 questions, each with a 15-second timer.
  • Scoring rewards speed + correctness.
  • Leaderboard submission is optional.

Question Topics

  • NFL History: iconic moments, legendary players, records, and throwbacks
  • Current Season: recent games, storylines, and relevant stats
  • Player Knowledge: careers, team changes, awards, and memorable plays
  • Fun Facts: surprising trivia and weird-but-true NFL details

Features

Daily Challenge

One new quiz per day keeps the leaderboard fair and makes Pigskin5 a simple daily ritual.

Streak Tracking

Pigskin5 tracks your streaks locally in your browser using local storage (no account needed). Keep a Daily Streak for consecutive plays and a Touchdown/Perfect Streak for 5/5 games.

Leaderboard Competition

Submit your score at the end to compete on the daily leaderboard. Rankings prioritize accuracy and points, with speed acting as a tie-breaker.

No Registration Required

No login. No account. Play instantly.

Quality & Accuracy

I take accuracy seriously. Questions are researched and reviewed before publishing, and if a mistake ever slips through, I want to fix it quickly — email me at pigskin5game@gmail.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Pigskin5?

Click “Start” on the homepage to begin today’s quiz. Answer 5 multiple-choice questions before the timer expires. After your results, you can submit your score to the leaderboard.

Can I play more than once per day?

No. Pigskin5 publishes one quiz per day to keep the leaderboard fair for everyone.

Does Pigskin5 collect personal data?

Pigskin5 does not require accounts. Your streaks/results are stored locally in your browser. If you submit a leaderboard score, we receive only what you submit (display name + score + timing). For details, read our Privacy Policy.

Is Pigskin5 affiliated with the NFL?

No. Pigskin5 is an independent fan-made project and is not endorsed by or affiliated with the NFL or any team. All NFL-related trademarks belong to their respective owners.

Support the Project

Pigskin5 is free to play. I plan to support hosting and development through advertising (including Google AdSense), so allowing ads helps keep the game running and improving.
