Pigskin 5

- Pigskin5 is a free daily NFL trivia game: you get 5 football questions with 15 seconds each to answer.
- Questions mix NFL history, current season storylines and statistics, iconic players, and fun random facts.
- Keep your Daily Streak alive, compete with friends, and try to score a Touchdown (a perfect 5/5).

Built for fun as a passion project by Twillyallen.

FAQs

How do I play Pigskin5?

5 NFL trivia questions, each with a 15-second timer. Select your answer before the clock runs out.

Can I play more than once per day?

No! Each day has one unique quiz. This keeps the game competitive and supports the daily leaderboard.

What kinds of questions appear?

Questions come from NFL history, current season stats, memorable players, and surprising fun facts.

Does Pigskin5 track my data?

No personal data is collected. Your streak and results are stored locally on your browser! See the Privacy Policy for details.

Is Pigskin5 affiliated with the NFL?

No. This site is a fan-made project and is not associated with the NFL or any NFL team.

Can I submit question ideas?

Yes! Feel free to reach out through the Contact Page.
