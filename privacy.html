Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 3, 2026

1) Who We Are

Pigskin5 is an independent fan-made NFL trivia game available at pigskin5.com. This site is operated by Thomas Allen (also known online as Twillyallen or @TwillysTakes) on X/Twitter.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

2) Quick Summary

  • No account required. You can play without creating an account or providing personal details.
  • Local game data stays on your device. Streaks and completion status are stored in your browser's 'local storage.
  • Leaderboard is optional. If you submit a score, we receive your (optional) display name + score + timing.
  • We use cookies. Cookies may be used for analytics and advertising (including Google AdSense).

3) Information We Collect

A) Information You Provide (Optional)

If you submit to the daily leaderboard, we collect:

  • Display name (the name you choose)
  • Quiz score
  • Average completion time (or similar timing metric)
  • Date of the daily quiz

We do not require email addresses, phone numbers, or real names for leaderboard submissions.

B) Local Storage (Stored on Your Device)

Pigskin5 uses your browser’s local storage to save game progress on your device. This may include:

  • Daily quiz completion status
  • Daily streak count
  • Touchdown/perfect-game streak count
  • Previous quiz results
  • Local settings or preferences (if applicable)

This local storage data is stored on your device and is not transmitted to us unless you choose to submit a score to the leaderboard. You can clear local storage at any time through your browser settings.

C) Automatically Collected Information (Standard Website Data)

Like most websites, when you visit Pigskin5 we (or our service providers) may automatically collect certain information, such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Approximate location (e.g., country/region derived from IP)
  • IP address
  • Pages viewed and time spent
  • Referring/exit pages
  • Date/time of access

4) Cookies, Consent, and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device. They help websites function, understand usage, and (in some cases) show relevant ads. We may also use similar technologies like local storage and pixels.

Cookie Consent

If we display a cookie consent banner on Pigskin5, you can use it to accept, reject, or manage non-essential cookies where required. You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Please note: disabling certain cookies may impact site functionality.

Types of Cookies We May Use

  • Essential cookies: needed to provide core site functionality.
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand how users interact with Pigskin5 (e.g., which pages are visited).
  • Advertising cookies: used to serve and measure ads (including interest-based ads where permitted).

5) Advertising (Google AdSense)

Pigskin5 may display ads through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the internet.

Google may use advertising cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to help serve and measure ads. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user’s prior visits to Pigskin5 or other websites.

Opting Out of Personalized Ads

You can manage ad personalization via:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page
  • Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page
